Vanilla Ice Cream

  • Time: 20min +freezing time
  • Makes: 1 1/4 quarts
Ingredients Quantity
Heavy Whiping Cream, Half and Half Cream, Sugar, Vanilla 2 C., 2 C., 1 C. 2 tsp

Step 1: Combine all ingreadiants and stir till sugar is melted
Step 2: Pour into container and freeze till firm. ENJOY!!!

Microwave Brownie

  • Time: 7min
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Flour, Sugar, Coco, oil, Water, Vanilla, Chocolate Chips 2 T, 2 T, 1 T, 1 T, 1 T, 1/2 tsp, 1-2 T

Step 1: Combine all ingreadiants and stir till smooth
Step 2:Put in microwave for 90 seconds. Let sit for 5min. ENJOY!!!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 15
  • Serves: 5-6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter, Sugar, Brown Sugar, Eggs, Vanilla, Salt, aking Soda, Flour, Chocolat Chips 2 cubes, 1 C., 1 C., 2 Eggs, 1 1/2 tsp, 1/2 tsp, 1 1/2 tsp, 2 C., 2C.

Step 1: Combine butter, sugar, brown sugar till smooth
Step 2:Add eggs, vanilla, salt, baking soda and mix till smooth
Step 3: Combine Flour and Chocolate Chips
Step 4: Cook in oven at 350 degrees for 8-10 min. ENJOY!!!
